Design Philosophy

I remember in the very first first lecture of my degree, Real Analysis with Professor Chris Good, he started with a rant about how we put so much effort into pedagogical research and then don't use it. He said that the most important thing in any lesson is motivation. Not the innate motivation of the students, but rather the teacher must explain why the contents of the lesson are worth learning before they are taught. I will try to follow this example, and start each page with motivation of why it is worth your time to read.
